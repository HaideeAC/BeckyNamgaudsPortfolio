ABOUT

Becky Namgauds is a choreographer and dancer based in London, creating powerful and visceral work.

Her choreography explores themes of human connection, nature, and movement through a unique physical language.

With a background in contemporary dance and somatic practices, her work has been presented at various venues and festivals.
